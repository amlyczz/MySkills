# 封面提示词 - agents-best-practices

## 3:4 比例

**English:**
Vertical 3:4 poster. Centered bold sans-serif text "AGENTS" in electric blue (#2563EB) with a dynamic slight italic tilt, subtitle "Runtime Discipline" in bright white below. Background: layered matte geometric planes in warm mango yellow (#F59E0B) and soft mint green (#6EE7B7), subtle paper texture with fine grain. Clean dramatic lighting from upper left creating soft shadows between the geometric layers. Text occupies central 1/3 of the frame. Generous whitespace, poster-like breathing room. Bold, sans-serif typography. No neon, no cyberpunk, no digital effects.

**中文：**
竖版 3:4 海报。画面正中央以电光蓝色（#2563EB）粗体无衬线字体呈现 "AGENTS"，带微妙的动态倾斜感，下方副标题 "Runtime Discipline" 以亮白色显示。背景是分层的哑光几何平面，使用温暖芒果黄（#F59E0B）和柔和薄荷绿（#6EE7B7），带有细腻纸张纹理和颗粒感。左上方干净戏剧性光线在几何层间投下柔和阴影。文字占据画面中央 1/3 区域。充足留白，具有海报般的呼吸感。粗体无衬线排版。无霓虹，无赛博朋克，无数字特效。

## 16:9 比例

**English:**
Horizontal 16:9 composition. Large centered bold sans-serif text "AGENT HARNESS" in vivid vermillion red (#DC2626), subtitle "Validate · Authorize · Execute" in bright white. Background: abstract architectural blueprint aesthetic in muted tones — soft graphite (#374151), warm cream (#FEF3C7), pale sage (#D1FAE5) — with fine grid lines and geometric annotations suggesting a control plane diagram. Dramatic chiaroscuro lighting from the right side, matte textures, no digital effects. Text occupies central 40% with generous negative space. Bold, sans-serif typography with dynamic weight.

**中文：**
横版 16:9 构图。画面正中央以朱红色（#DC2626）粗体无衬线字体呈现 "AGENT HARNESS"，副标题 "Validate · Authorize · Execute" 以亮白色显示。背景是抽象建筑蓝图风格——柔和石墨灰（#374151）、温暖奶油色（#FEF3C7）、淡鼠尾草绿（#D1FAE5）——带有精细网格线和几何标注，暗示控制平面图。右侧戏剧性明暗对比光影，哑光质感，无数字特效。文字占据画面中央 40%，周围大量留白。粗体无衬线排版，字重富有动态感。

**视觉注意**：均使用亮色、颗粒质感、优雅构图，无霓虹/赛博朋克元素。
